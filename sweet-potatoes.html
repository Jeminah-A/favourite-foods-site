Sweet Potatoes - A Nutrient Powerhouse

Sweet potatoes are loaded with vitamins and antioxidants to support a healthy body.

Health Benefits

Sweet potatoes are rich in beta-carotene, vitamin A, and antioxidants. They improve vision, boost immunity, and promote healthy skin.

Type of Sweet Potatoes

Sweet potatoes come in different colors: orange, purple, and white. Each type has unique flavors and nutritional benefits.

Preparation Methods

Sweet potatoes can be boiled, roasted, mashed, or baked. They are delicious in dishes like baked sweet potato fries, casseroles, and stews.
