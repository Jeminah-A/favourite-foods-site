Sweet Potatoes - A Nutritious Delight

Sweet potatoes are an excellent source of vitamins and minerals, with many delicious ways to prepare them.

Health Benefits

Sweet potatoes are rich in vitamins A and C, potassium, and dietary fibre. They promote good vision, strengthen the immune system, and help regulate blood sugar levels.

Types of Sweet Potatoes

There are various types of sweet potatoes, ranging from orange to purple flesh. The most common variety is the orange-fleshed sweet potato, known for its sweet flavour and vibrant colour.

Preparation Methods

Sweet potatoes can be prepared in many ways: boiled, baked, mashed, or fried. They are versatile and can be served in both savoury and sweet dishes.

Popular Ghanaian Sweet Potato Dishes

Fried Sweet Potatoes

Fried sweet potatoes are a popular snack or side dish in Ghana. The sweet potatoes are sliced and deep-fried until golden and crispy. They are often enjoyed with spicy pepper sauce or as a snack with fried fish.

Roasted Sweet Potatoes

Roasted sweet potatoes are another favourite Ghanaian dish. The sweet potatoes are simply wrapped in foil and roasted over an open flame or in the oven. This simple preparation brings out the natural sweetness of the potatoes, making it a popular street food.
