Maize - The Healthy Staple

Maize is a nutritious, versatile crop packed with benefits for your health.

Health Benefits

Maize is high in fiber, antioxidants, and essential vitamins. It promotes heart health, boosts the immune system, and helps regulate blood sugar levels.

Type of Maize

There are different types of maize, including sweet corn, field corn, and popcorn. Each type has different uses, such as cooking, oil production, or snacks.

Preparation Methods

Maize can be boiled, grilled, or made into cornmeal for baking. It's commonly used in dishes like corn on the cob, tortillas, and polenta.
